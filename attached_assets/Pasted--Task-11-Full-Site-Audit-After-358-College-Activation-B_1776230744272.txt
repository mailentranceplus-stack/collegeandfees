## Ultimate Objective — Why This Site Exists

CollegeAndFees.com is an SEO-first engineering college directory. The entire business model is:

1. **Rank on Google + AI search** (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) for queries like "RVCE management quota fees 2026", "IIT Bombay fees", "BITS Pilani direct admission", "best engineering colleges in Bangalore"
2. **Get parents & students on the site** — every page must load fast, show real data, and look professional
3. **Convert to WhatsApp leads** — every page should guide visitors to click WhatsApp (wa.me/917975193033)

**More pages with real data = more SEO rankings = more traffic = more WhatsApp leads.**

We now have **358 colleges** with **8,877 real fee rows** across **6 quotas** and **28 branches**, plus **1,790 FAQs** and **358 content entries**. Every college should have a complete, functional set of 4 pages: overview, fees, management-quota, and direct-admission. The site should feel like a polished, trustworthy resource — not a half-built prototype.
